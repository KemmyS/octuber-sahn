WebApp Dashboard

A dashboard that summarizes a user's activities such as notifications, data reports and many more. I used Chart.js to create the information for the Web Traffic and Mobile User chart widgets. Interacting with each chart widget gives you a specific insight about it. User's can also view notifications by clicking on the bell icon. User's settings are saved to local storage and removed when they click cancel.

A word guessing game for common phrases. Players can guess a random phrase by clicking letters from an onscreen keyboard. Correct letters will appear in the phrase, and incorrect guesses will be indicated by the removal of a heart icon. Players can reset the game after a win or loss.
